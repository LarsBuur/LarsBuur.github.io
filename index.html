I am an Internet professional with 19 years experience in software engineering, web programming and e-commerce consulting.

I have build two companies of some notion and helped hundreds of companies with their web related challenges ranging from top 100 US companies to small one-person shops just starting out.

In late 1996 I started the company Snabel & Co in Copenhagen. We were a “normal” web-agency and we build static/dynamic webpages for huge Danish companies and institutions. We quickly decided to build a backend system to enable editors to maintain the content on their websites. Something that later was known as CMS. We build a product called “Site In A Box” and it was the first CMS in Denmark.
